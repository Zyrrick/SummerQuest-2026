There was a little dragon who was very sad. He wanted to be big and strong and happy like the other animals, but he was stuck in a hole. He had no friends to play with, and he was very sad.
One day, a little girl named Lily saw the dragon. She thought the dragon was very lonely and wanted to help him. Lily went to the dragon and said, "Hello, Mr. Dragon! Why do you look so sad?" The dragon replied, "I am lonely, and I have no friends. No one wants to be my friend."
Lily felt sorry for the dragon and wanted to help him. She said, "I will be your friend, and we can play together." The dragon was very happy and agreed to play with Lily. They played all day and had lots of fun. But then, something unexpected happened. The dragon began to change! He became a beautiful dragon with wings and a beautiful light.
The dragon and Lily became the best of friends. They played together every day and had lots of fun. The dragon was not lonely anymore. He had found a friend and a beautiful dragon, just like Lily's. They were the best of friends forever.
