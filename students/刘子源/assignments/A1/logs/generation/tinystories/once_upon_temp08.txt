Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lucy. She had a very special box that she found in her room. It was a very special box, and it was the only one she had with it. She wanted to know what was inside, so she tried to open it. But it was locked.
One day, her mom said, "Lucy, you need to be patient and wait your turn." But Lucy didn't want to wait. She wanted to see what was in the box. She asked, "Mom, can I open the box now?"
Her mom said, "No, Lucy. It's not a good idea. You must wait a little bit and wait." Lucy was sad but she listened to her mom. She waited until her mom was not looking and took the box to the other room.
When she opened the box, she found a toy car inside. Lucy was so happy! She played with the car and forgot about the lock. When it was time to open the box, Lucy learned that it's important to be patient and wait for the right time. And the moral of the story is to be patient and wait for the right time.
