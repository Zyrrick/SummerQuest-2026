Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She had a big, red ball. Lily loved to play with her ball in the park. One day, she saw a small, helpless bird on the ground. The bird could not fly.
Lily wanted to help the bird. She had an idea. She would use her big, red ball to make the bird fly. She put the ball on the ground and threw it up. The ball started to rise up into the air.
The bird saw the ball and wanted to play too. It flew down and landed on the ground. The bird picked up the ball with its beak and flew away. Lily was happy that the bird was free. She played with her ball all day long.
